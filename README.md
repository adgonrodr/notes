We provide two scripts to facilitate metadata collection from Collibra and generate data contracts:

1. Collibra to Data Contract

This script collects physical metadata from Collibra and generates a data contract template.
	•	The template simplifies the initial creation of the data contract.

Additional Steps:
	1.	Enrich the data contract with:
	•	Business metadata, such as business descriptions and glossaries.
	•	Data quality rules, if required.
	2.	Review the data contract to ensure accuracy and completeness.

2. Collibra Intake Form to Data Contract

This script collects both business and physical metadata from the Collibra intake form. The intake form must be completed as part of the Collibra workflow to propose a data product.
	•	The script generates a comprehensive data contract based on the intake form.

Additional Steps:
	1.	Validate and enhance the data contract by:
	•	Adding entity business descriptions (not included in the intake form).
	•	Incorporating data quality rules and policies.
	•	Ensuring alignment with organizational standards.
